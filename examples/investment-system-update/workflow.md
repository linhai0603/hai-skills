# Demo Workflow: How The Skill Curates The Sources

## Diagnosis

The new notes do not merely add more bullets. They suggest a clearer structure:

- `Buy Good Assets At Good Prices` should keep the price discipline idea.
- `Risk Control` should separate permanent loss from normal volatility.
- `Margin Of Safety` deserves its own second-level section because multiple new notes define what "cheap enough" means.
- Repeated ideas about price, patience, and volatility should be merged rather than appended.

| "The biggest risk is pretending to understand..." | Supplements risk section | Risk control |

## Integration Rules Applied

- Do not create duplicate sections for similar claims.
- Preserve strong source wording as bullets.
- Move examples and warnings under the principle they support.
- Keep the final structure easy to update later.
