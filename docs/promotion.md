## Chinese Long Version

很多知识库的问题不是信息太少，而是新资料不断进来以后，旧结构越来越乱：同一个观点重复出现，重要表达被摘要掉，新内容不知道应该放在哪里。

所以我做了一个 Codex Skill：`knowledge-system-curator`。

它不是简单总结，也不是把新内容追加到文档末尾。它会先读已有体系，再拆解新增资料，判断每条内容和原体系的关系：

- 是补充已有内容
- 是重复但有差异
- 是完全重复
- 是会触发结构调整
- 是冲突或张力
- 是案例或证据

对于复杂文档，它会先给诊断和执行计划，等确认后再修改。默认只基于你提供的资料，不新增外部内容。

适合：

- 投资体系整理
- 读书摘录整合
- 飞书/Lark 知识库更新
- 长期研究框架维护
- 个人知识管理

GitHub: https://github.com/linhai0603/hai-skills

## English Long Version

Most knowledge bases do not fail because there is too little information. They fail because new material keeps arriving, categories drift, duplicate ideas pile up, and important wording gets lost during summarization.

`knowledge-system-curator` is a Codex Skill for maintaining living knowledge systems.

It helps Codex:

- Read the existing document before editing.
- Break new material into knowledge units.
- Classify each unit as supplement, duplicate, structure trigger, conflict, evidence, or out of scope.
- Propose a diagnosis before changing complex documents.
- Integrate new material into the right place.
- Preserve important source wording.
- Validate coverage, structure, formatting, and source boundaries.

Useful for personal knowledge bases, research systems, book notes, investment frameworks, Feishu/Lark docs, and long-form system documents.

GitHub: https://github.com/linhai0603/hai-skills
