# investor-company-xray

Investment Company X-Ray helps long-term investors examine whether a company deserves serious research attention before discussing whether the stock is cheap.

It checks business model, governance, management quality, moat, pricing power, financial health, valuation caveats, industry context, and evidence gaps.

This skill is for educational decision support only. It does not provide stock picks, price forecasts, buy/sell/hold recommendations, or personalized financial advice.

## Files

- `SKILL.md`: Codex skill instructions and trigger rules.
- `references/`: company-quality review framework.
- `templates/`: reusable output structure.
- `examples/`: fictional usage example.
- `agents/`: local skill metadata.
